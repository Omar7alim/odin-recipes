pring Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rice vermicelli noodles
  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 8 large cooked shrimp, peeled and halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves(optional if u dont like them)
  • 3 leaves lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare Noodles: Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil. Add rice noodles and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. Soften Wrappers: Fill a large bowl with warm water. Dip one wrapper into the water for 1 second to soften. Lay wrapper flat on a clean surface.
  3. Layer Ingredients: In the center of the wrapper, place 2 shrimp halves, a handful of noodles, lettuce, mint, and cilantro.
  4. Roll it up: Fold sides of the wrapper inward, then tightly roll the wrapper starting from the bottom.
  5. Serve: Repeat with remaining ingredients and serve immediately with dipping sauce.
