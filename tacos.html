Authentic Street Tacos

Description

Soft corn tortillas filled with seasoned meat, topped with fresh white onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice. Simple, fast, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak or chicken breast
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp chili powder

Steps

  1. Season Meat: Rub the meat with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Grill: Cook meat over medium-high heat until well-browned. Let it rest for 5 minutes, then dice into small bits.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Heat tortillas on a dry skillet until soft and slightly charred.
  4. Assemble: Place meat on tortillas and top with onions and cilantro.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side.
