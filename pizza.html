Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

A thin-crust Italian classic featuring a simple tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. It’s the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat: Set your oven to its highest setting (usually 500°F/260°C).
  2. Shape Dough: Stretch the dough onto a floured surface until thin and round.
  3. Add Toppings: Spread sauce evenly, then add mozzarella slices.
  4. Bake: Slide onto a pizza stone or baking sheet and cook for 8-10 minutes until crust is golden.
  5. Finish: Remove from oven, add fresh basil, and drizzle with olive oil.
